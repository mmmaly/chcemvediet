Why do I have to register?

Registration is necessary for your comfort and also for the necessary formalities:

  • Every inforequest must include the postal address.
  • In case of refusal, the institution will send a written resolution, not an email.
  • If you would send an appeal, that must be in a written form (yes, really, on a paper -- we did not made that up).
